About

There's the tale of a blind, the life of a beautiful rose but painted with black.
The eyes were beautiful but covered with the darkest veil.
The path of throns and the dark which never left.
It's not easy to be blind and it's never been easy that way.
It was never been their choice and none of it was a fancy.
But we can be the light they wished for, We can be the strength
and we can be the beautiful song that fills the heart
So as this project aims to.
